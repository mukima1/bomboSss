REQUIREMENTS ON REPORTING

Documents Required:
  • Original Admission Letter
  • 03 recent passport photographs (black & white) – one for a parent
  • Previous school academic documents (report cards, certificates)
  • Birth certificate or national ID (copy)
  • Transfer documents (for transfer students)
Stationery Requirements:
  • Students' scholastic materials: Books, Rulers, Geometry set
  • 5 pens and 5 pencils
  • 3 Graph books
  • One ream of photocopy/plain paper (500 sheets) – S1, S2 & S3 termly
  • One ream of ruled paper – S4, S5 & S6 termly
General Requirements:
  • One compound broom & inside broom – termly
  • Squeezer / brush – S1, S5 & new students annually
  • A 20-litre capacity jerry can, bucket & basin – for boarding students only
  • Bedding (mattress, bedsheets, blanket) – for boarding students only
  • Personal toiletry items – for boarding students only

PAYMENT INFORMATION

  • All payments should be made to the school bank account or at the school bursar's office
  • Payment receipts must be kept safely and presented when required
  • Partial payments may be accepted under special arrangements with the administration
  • Students with outstanding fees will not be allowed to sit for examinations
  • Fee structure is subject to change with prior notice from the school administration
