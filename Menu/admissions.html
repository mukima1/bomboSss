Bombo Senior Secondary School welcomes students from all nationalities and religious backgrounds. Admission is based on academic performance and fulfillment of our requirements.

Admission Requirements

S.1 Admission

Based on Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) Results:

  • Students with Grade I or II
  • Selected through UNEB or school selection

S.5 Admission

Based on Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) Results:

  • Meeting UNEB criteria
  • Subject to school capacity

Islamic Theology

Special program for:

  • Students with IPLE results
  • Beginners in Islamic studies
