Admissions & Fees Structure

Quality education for national development. Explore our admission requirements and transparent fee structure for the upcoming academic year.

Admission Requirements

Senior 1 (S.1) Admission

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) Certificate
  • Grade I or II in PLE results
  • UNEB selection number required
  • Parent/guardian interview
  • Medical examination report

Senior 5 (S.5) Admission

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)
  • Minimum 28 points in UCE
  • Subject combination requirements
  • Based on school capacity
  • Transfer documents for external candidates

General Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • 3 recent passport photos
  • Birth certificate/National ID copy
  • Previous school reports
  • Recommendation letter
  • Medical report from recognized facility
