CPA Samuel Opio

Chairperson, Bombo SS Old Student's Association (BOSA)

As the Chairperson of the Bombo Senior Secondary School Alumni Association, it is with great pleasure that I extend a warm welcome to you, our esteemed stakeholders. Whether you recently completed or have been a valued member of our alumni network for many years, we are thrilled to have you join us in this virtual space where we can connect, engage, and continue to support one another in our shared commitment to our alma mater.

As most of you are aware, the magic that both you and I had the honor of being enlightened with at the school (which certainly varies as the school has gone through a number of setbacks over the years) has been under threat for quite some time. As Bombo SS's Alumni/Strength, we have been looking for ways to keep the magic alive for years to come. As the present Executive Committee, we have established our vision, mission, and objectives. As the Strength, we continue to be the pillars of this Bombo SS, a true beacon of light in an otherwise dark world. No doubt if there is anyone capable of reigniting the daring and maverick spirit of holistic brilliance, it should be us.

Our time there gave us the knowledge, abilities, and confidence to believe that we can be whatever we want to be. It is within the confines of this prestigious school that both staff and fellow students have given me the confidence to take up any leadership mantle thrown at me with a sense of responsibility and the understanding that the 'student' body plays an important role in the continued success of this special school mentored me. The spill-over benefits of those who came before us undoubtedly left a lasting impression on the school's memory long after your departure, becoming not only part of the school's oral traditions, but also an integral element of the school's distinct DNA.

To our Headteacher

We appreciate the leadership and vision you bring to the school, fostering an environment of academic excellence and personal growth. The alumni are grateful for the strong foundation laid during our time at school and are committed to supporting the school's ongoing development, and recognize the efforts of the staff and students and are eager to collaborate on initiatives that benefit the school; we commend your dedication to nurturing talented individuals and continued partnership.

To our dear Students

We have walked the paths you have started on and understand the excitement and challenges of your academic journey. We are proud of your achievements and inspired by your potential.

We encourage you to work diligently, pursue your passions, and make the most of the opportunities available to you. Stay curious, be resilient, and support one another ‚Äì these are keys to success, we are here to support you, share our experiences, and celebrate your accomplishments. Believe in yourselves and strive for excellence.

The Board of Governors

We appreciate your stewardship and commitment to guiding the school towards continued excellence and growth. The alumni value the strategic direction and oversight you provide, which enables Bombo SS to maintain high standards of education and foster a supportive community. We are proud of the school's achievements and are committed to collaborating with the Board and the school administration to support initiatives that benefit Bombo SS.

We value the contributions of parents, teachers, local leaders, and partners who work together to create a conducive learning environment. The alumni are committed to collaborating with stakeholders to enhance the school's growth, promote excellence, and foster strong relationships.

Together, we can build on Bombo SS's strengths and address challenges to ensure the school continues to thrive and produce capable, responsible citizens.

As we set out to revitalize the school's illustrious tradition of excellence, we extend a warm invitation to you to join us in an effort to rebuild our school and ensure that we keep generating tomorrow's leaders, for which many of you are living example. Let us continue to uphold the values and excellence of our institution, working together to make a positive impact. We encourage you to stay engaged, share your experiences, and support current students and fellow alumni. Your involvement strengthens our network and inspires the next generation.

We appreciate the dedication of the staff and the opportunities provided to us. We remain committed to giving back, supporting current students, and contributing to the school's continued growth and success.

Thank you for your support during this time; we are committed to keeping you informed. Let us embrace this period with courage and conviction. Together, we have the power to leave a lasting impact on generations to come. I am honored to serve as your alumni Chairperson and I look forward to the journey ahead.

"The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams."

- Eleanor Roosevelt

Indeed, the future of Bombo SS is in our hands.

Thank you.

CPA Samuel Opio

Chairperson, Bombo SS Old Student's Association (BOSA)
