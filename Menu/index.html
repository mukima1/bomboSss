Organizational Structure Report

Governance & Executive Administration
Board of Governors (B.O.G)

The Supreme Governing Body representing the Ministry of Education. Responsible for strategic planning, policy approval, and ensuring compliance with government guidelines. Their composition is reviewed every three years.

Chairman: Dr. Ahmed Musa (P.H.D)
Parents Teachers Association (P.T.A)

Acts as the key development partner, mobilizing parents for essential school infrastructure projects, fee collection support, and bridging communication between home and school.

Chairman: Mr. Shafi Muzamil
Head Teacher (CEO / Accounting Officer)

The chief executive officer and accounting officer, responsible for the implementation of B.O.G policies, managing all staff (teaching and non-teaching), and the daily administration of the school.

Hajji Lubega Musa Matovu
Key Administrative & Academic Management
Deputy Head Teacher (Administration)

Manages the school's non-academic operations, including student welfare, discipline, physical maintenance of facilities, and oversight of games and sports.

Deputy Head Teacher (Academics)

Monitors teaching quality, teacher evaluation, coordinates external examinations, and ensures the successful execution of skilling and curriculum programs.

Director of Studies (D.O.S)

Responsible for the day-to-day academic workflow, including the creation and management of the teaching timetable, scheduling internal examinations, and student class placement. Supported by two assistants.

Heads of Department (H.O.D)

Departmental heads manage subject-specific resources, mentor teachers, and ensure consistent curriculum delivery across their disciplines.

Academic Departments:
  • Biological Sciences
  • Physical Sciences
  • Vocational Sciences
  • Humanities
  • Languages
Class Teachers

Teachers assigned to individual classes, serving as the first point of contact for student performance, welfare, and discipline matters. They compile learner reports.

Student Governance & Leadership
Student Leadership Structure

The student body is represented through two main leadership organs that liaise with the administration for student welfare and conduct.
